MiTLI

Mission to Transform Lives International(MiTLI) is a non-partisan, non-profit civil empowered organization focused on the delivery of essential themes necessary for the well-being and poverty alleviation in the rural communities.

Our Vision

MiTLI seeks to promote a healthy living environment and livelihood empowerment to the vulnerable and the marginalized in Ghana.

Our Mission

MiTLI is a well renowned Ghana-based Non-Governmental Organization that seeks to promote public health, environmental sanitation services, women and girls right, self-development within marginalized groups and communities irrespective of their religious affiliation, race, ethnicity or gender among others aim at building their capabilities to provide for themselves and solve their own Socio-economic problems.

Our Objectives

The main objectives of which Mission to Transform Lives International (MiTLI) is formed include:

  • Sensitizing the public against HIV/AIDs prevalence
  • Provision of potable water and sanitation services
  • Promoting health education on environmental sanitation and hygiene activities
  • Promoting adolescent sexual reproductive health
  • Promoting the right and liberty of the girl child
  • Provision of livelihood empowerment programmes through vocational and entrepreneurial training
